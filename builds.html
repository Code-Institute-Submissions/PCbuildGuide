This is a "budget" PC. The word budget is in quotes because there are cheaper alternatives, however this setup will run all and any of your games at 1080p without an issue! It is rocking a very solid and crowd's facourite gaming CPU, a Ryzen R5 3600. It is a 6 core 12 thread beast that will absolutely fit any gamer's needs given that a game is the only task assigned to it at a given time. GTX 1660 Super is possibly the most affordable and reliable card (If we consider NEW parts only) to suit a 1920x1080 monitor's resolution. The horsepower, low power draw and affordability is why it has been chosen for this build. Following, we have 16GB of Corsai Vengeance RAM. This particular RAM has proven itself among Ryzen 3000 series and above users to overclock well and hold it's ground due to its price/performance ratio. Lastly, it rocks 240 GB SSD which is more than enough to house your Operating System of choice and perhaps a game or two of your choosing while also allowing for bigger and slower storage on the side.

My chosen mid-range PC build. I define mid-range by the computer's capability to run most modern games at 2560x1440p 144Hz without drawbacks and this is it! Once again, I am back to Ryzen line of processors as they are the best in the business for productivity and gaming at the same time without competition. This time, it is an 8 core 16 thread champion that can handle anything you throw at it! For the most exciting part of the build, I have chosen the newly released RTX 3070. It's price to performance ratio is unmatched, and although it is not cheap you will not think twice before running any sort of game on it. Again, talking about RAM I have chosen 16GB of RAM because techincally, you do not need any more unless you are a chrome tab hoarder or a 3D designer. For the last piece of notable hardware in this build, its the 256GB SSD which is standard for your Operating System etc and a 2TB hard drive to store all the games you need (Let's not talk about CoD).

This build is an absolute overkill for any kind of game out there, unless you are rocking 4K144Hz monitor setup, there is no way you can make this computer struggle at all. The last build on display is the end all, spend all machine. The processor boasts 12 cores and 24 threads turbo-boosting up to 4.8GHz all-core out of the box, need I say more? I have chosen RTX 3080 graphics card, it is not the highest performing card out in the wild, under RTX 3090/RX 6800XT and equal to RX 6800 but afterall, what would you need more performance for?. RAM is standard as it is for the other two builds, although this time since it is a "luxury" build, I have added 32GB instead of 16GB because why not!. Finally, the storage. Primary storage is an M.2 SSD that is running on Gen4 speeds which is only attainable via AMD platform currently. Boasting speeds twice as fast as regular m.2 SSD. Oh yeah, and 1TB SSD because we can!
